# Pixel Sampler — Store Listing

## Title
Pixel Sampler

## Subtitle / Tagline
Fonts · Colors · Platform — all on your wrist.

## Description

The Pebble developer's on-wrist reference.

Building a Pebble app and need to check what LECO 38 Bold looks like, or grab the hex code for GColorVividCerulean? Stop switching windows. Pixel Sampler puts everything you need right on your watch.

**Fonts** — Browse all 25 Pebble system fonts (23 on older platforms). Every font is shown in the list at a glance. Tap any font for a scrollable specimen page with sample strings: time, digits, letters, text, symbols. UP/DOWN navigates between fonts — when you hit the bottom of one specimen, the next font loads automatically.

**Colors** — The full 64-color Pebble palette in spectrum order. Each row shows a color swatch and name. Tap any color to see the full screen filled with it, along with the GColor constant name, hex code, and 2-bit RGB channel values. UP/DOWN scrolls through all colors without going back to the list. B&W platforms show the 4 actual gray levels.

**Platform** — Your current device at a glance: platform codename, watch model, screen dimensions, shape, color depth, and health capabilities.

All 7 Pebble platforms supported: Aplite, Basalt, Chalk, Diorite, Emery, Flint, Gabbro.
